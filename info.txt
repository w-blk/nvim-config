### for update: :Lazy and U

### leader - space

Find:
    - find file **leader-ff**
    - find grep **leader-fg**

Neo-tree:
    - Show left **leader-nl**
    - Show right **leader-nr**
    - Show float **leader-nf**

Terminal:
    - Show/hide **Ctrl-\**
    - Show float **leader-tf**
    - Show vertical **leader-tv**
    - Show horizontal **leader-th**
